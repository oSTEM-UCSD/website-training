Together in Community A Little About Us

oSTEM, or Out in Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics, is a professional development student organization geared towards STEM majors in the LGBTQ+ Community. At oSTEM, we hope to create a safe space for, provide resources to, and develop the skills of our members. oSTEM hosts General Body Meetings ranging from professional development days to guest speaker events, Social events with various organizations, Study Jams at the end of each quarter, and many other fun, exciting events!If you are interested in getting involved with oSTEM, please feel free to attend our Welcome GBM and other events as membership is open to all.
